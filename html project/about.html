About Us


Garden of Lizie was founded because I thought of converting my pleasure a business, as I kept taking care of my plants, I soon realize that there were others who are ready to acquire the plants that I took care of. Since home gardening is getting more and more popular due to the mandated quarantine laws of the government. People are required to stay inside of their houses and seek for stuff to accomplish while they safely wait for the number of occurrences to go down. We supply a variety of plants and we aspire to be a significant producer of all sorts of plants to the market, from selling seeds to selling magnificent blossoms and such.
